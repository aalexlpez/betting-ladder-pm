## Implement

- Hero section with terminal mockup.
- Value cards.
- Competitor wedge section.
- Safety and live-gate section.
- Workflow section.
- CTA and legal note.

## Forbidden

- No live trading code.
- No credentials.
- No fake testimonials/logos/statistics.
- No profit claims.
- No legal certainty claims.
- No geoblock evasion language.

## Acceptance criteria

- Landing feels product-specific and credible.
- It communicates desktop terminal, ladder trading, local/no-custody posture, and live gates.
- It is visually stronger than a generic AI SaaS page.
- It builds successfully or blockers are documented.
- `docs/ai/context-handoff.md` is updated.
