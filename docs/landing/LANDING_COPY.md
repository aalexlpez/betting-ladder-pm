## Value cards

### Ladder-first execution

See prices, bid/ask liquidity, spread, and order intent in one dense trading surface built for quick manual decisions.

### Local-first posture

The product is designed so credentials and signing can stay local. No cloud custody and no hidden broker layer.

### Risk-gated live mode

Paper and dry-run modes support development and QA. Live trading requires explicit approval, limits, geoblock checks, kill switch, and audit logging.

## Problem section

Native prediction-market interfaces are built for broad participation. Serious order-book traders need a different surface: price ladder, fast stake controls, visible liquidity, quick cancellation, and fewer misclick paths.

## Wedge section

### Not another dashboard

Charts are useful, but execution happens at the ladder. Prediction Ladder is designed around the order book first.

### Not an opaque bot

The first product is for manual trading. No hidden strategies, no copied signals, no automated promises.

### Not custody-first

The product direction is local-first and no-custody. If live trading is enabled, credentials and signing must pass explicit safety gates.

## Safety section

Live trading is powerful and dangerous. The app treats live execution as an explicit mode, not a default. Every live path must pass configured limits, legal approval, geoblock checks, credential checks, and audit logging.

## Workflow section

1. Select a live market.
2. Inspect the ladder and available liquidity.
3. Choose stake and execution mode.
4. Arm live trading only when gates pass.
5. Place, monitor, and cancel orders with a local audit trail.

## Fair pricing section

The controlled pilot is free. Read-only data, paper trading, and live-dry-run remain available for evaluation. Future pricing is planned around disclosed provider-approved fees or revenue share where allowed, with subscription as a fallback. Any paid routing, if enabled later, must be disclosed before order submission.

## Legal note

Prediction markets and event contracts may be restricted in some jurisdictions. This product must not be used to evade geoblocks, KYC, platform restrictions, or applicable law. Live execution requires human approval and jurisdiction-specific review.

## Footer CTA

Built for a controlled pilot first. Public availability depends on legal, platform, and launch-readiness gates.
