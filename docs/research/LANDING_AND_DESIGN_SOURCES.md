# Landing and Design Sources

External resources are inputs, not source-of-truth. Project ADRs, product docs, and legal gates override them.

## Approved references

- Anthropic `frontend-design`: useful for distinctive, production-grade frontend design and avoiding generic AI/SaaS aesthetics; not sufficient for legal/product positioning.
- Vercel `web-design-guidelines`: useful as a post-implementation UI/accessibility/performance review skill.
- Copywriting and page-CRO skills: useful as references for clarity and CTA sequencing; local product/legal constraints override them.
- shadcn/ui: useful for accessible React/Tailwind building blocks; must be customized heavily.
- Launch UI: structural landing-page inspiration; do not copy generic SaaS messaging.
- Aceternity UI: selective motion/background inspiration; use sparingly.
- Competitor pages: public positioning claims only; not proof of traction, quality, maturity, or legality.

## Rejected usage

- No copied competitor layouts.
- No fake testimonials, logos, usage counts, security badges, or trading volume.
- No generic AI gradients, crypto-casino imagery, or abstract dashboard visuals that do not show the ladder.
