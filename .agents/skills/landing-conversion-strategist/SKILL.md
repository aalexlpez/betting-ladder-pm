# Landing Conversion Strategist

Use this before implementing or rewriting the landing page. The goal is not to make the page pretty; the goal is to make the product position sharp, credible, and differentiated.

## Product truth

- Product: Windows desktop trading terminal for prediction market ladders.
- Providers: Polymarket and Kalshi, with provider-aware support and careful legal availability language.
- Execution: live-ready with hard gates; paper/live-dry-run as safety harness and fallback.
- User: active prediction market traders and exchange-style traders.
- Core value: faster manual order book interaction with risk controls.
- Legal posture: live trading only where permitted and approved.
- Custody posture: no server-side custody of user funds, seed phrases, or private keys.

## Copy rules

Use:

- “live-ready” instead of “fully legal everywhere”;
- “guarded one-click” instead of “risk-free one-click”;
- “local-first credentials” instead of “we store your API keys safely”;
- “where permitted” instead of “available globally”.

Do not use profit guarantees, “safe from regulation”, “avoid blocks”, “bypass restrictions”, casino language, fake customer logos, or fake usage metrics.
